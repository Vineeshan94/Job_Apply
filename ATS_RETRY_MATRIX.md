# ATS Preparation Notes

Use this as a lightweight checklist for preparing application materials across common ATS platforms.

## General

- Prefer official company or ATS application URLs.
- Upload the best matching resume from `resumes/`.
- If upload parsing is poor, prepare manual-entry text from the resume.
- Save recruiter outreach as Gmail drafts unless Vineesha explicitly asks to send.
- Submit applications after required fields are truthfully complete and no hard-stop blocker is present.

## Greenhouse / Lever / Ashby

- Confirm the selected resume filename is Vineesha's current resume.
- Fill draft answers from `ANSWER_BANK.md` and the job description.
- Record CAPTCHA, private-info, legal, suspicious-job, or assessment blockers in the tracker.

## Workday / SuccessFactors / Oracle

- These portals may ask for structured profile, education, work authorization, and demographic fields.
- Prepare only non-sensitive information from `APPLICATION_PLAN.md`.
- Stop for private identity fields, legal attestations, account credentials, CAPTCHA, suspicious forms, or payment requests.

## LinkedIn / Indeed

- Use for discovery and draft preparation.
- Submit Easy Apply only after preflight and required-answer review.
- Do not change saved account data without Vineesha review.
