## Safety Rules

- Do not store passwords, Gmail secrets, API keys, tokens, private credentials, DOB, SSN, passport, or payment data in this repository.
- Use `.env.example` only. Do not create or commit a real `.env`.
- Applications may be submitted after the role passes preflight and the required fields can be answered truthfully from this repo, the resume, the job description, LinkedIn/GitHub profile context, or normal safe judgment.
- Create Gmail drafts for recruiter outreach and follow-up messages; do not send outreach emails automatically unless Vineesha explicitly asks in the active run.
- Do not complete legal attestations, background checks, identity checks, assessments, or forms requiring private facts unless Vineesha explicitly provides the answer.
- Keep tracker notes free of private credentials, email codes, and sensitive identity values.

## Operating Flow

1. Read `APPLICATION_PLAN.md`, `ANSWER_BANK.md`, and `TRACKER_SCHEMA.md`.
2. Confirm the role is real, open, and relevant. Do not filter out otherwise fitting roles only because they mention visa sponsorship, no sponsorship, or immigration support.
3. Select the best resume from the `resumes/` folder.
4. Draft answers, cover letters, recruiter emails, and follow-ups using the resume and job description.
5. Submit the application when all required fields are truthfully complete and no hard-stop blocker is present.
6. Check Gmail for confirmation proof when practical.
7. Update `JOB_APPLICATION_TRACKER.xlsx` with submitted, already-applied, skipped, drafted, or blocked status.
8. Save recruiter outreach as Gmail drafts unless Vineesha explicitly asks to send.
