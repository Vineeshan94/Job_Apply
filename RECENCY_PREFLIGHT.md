# Recency And Fit Preflight

Use this before preparing an application or Gmail draft.

## Required Checks

- Confirm the role is open on an official company or ATS page.
- Prefer roles posted within the last 15 days.
- Confirm the role matches one of Vineesha's target tracks: Java/full-stack/backend/cloud or AI/data/ML.
- Confirm the location is compatible with Atlanta, GA, remote, hybrid, or a location Vineesha has approved.
- Do not skip a good role only because it mentions visa sponsorship, no sponsorship, immigration support, or sponsorship-related questions.
- Confirm the role does not require citizenship, clearance, export-control status, or private eligibility that Vineesha has not confirmed.
- Check `JOB_APPLICATION_TRACKER.xlsx` for duplicates before preparing materials.

## 24-Hour Rapid Window

When Vineesha asks for urgent monitoring, check frequently for newly posted matching roles for the next 24 hours. Prioritize:

- Roles posted today or within the last hour.
- Roles with visible low applicant counts, especially under 10 applicants.
- Official company or ATS application pages where submission can be completed quickly and truthfully.
- Strong matches across Java Full Stack, backend, AI, ML, Data Scientist, Generative AI, LLM, RAG, and data platform roles.

Do not submit stale, scam-like, irrelevant, low-quality, duplicate, or unsafe roles just to increase volume.

## Skip Reasons

Skip roles that are closed, stale, obvious scams, low-quality staffing/vendor posts, duplicate, unrelated to the target tracks, or require private facts that are not available. Do not use sponsorship or no-sponsorship wording alone as a skip reason.

## Submission Standard

If the role passes preflight and no hard-stop blocker is present, submit the application when required fields can be answered truthfully from this repo, the resume, the job description, LinkedIn/GitHub context, or safe judgment. Save recruiter outreach as a Gmail draft unless Vineesha explicitly asks to send.
